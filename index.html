About Us

Dedicated to Your Complete Recovery

Welcome to PhysioGlides Physiotherapy Clinic, your trusted destination for expert physiotherapy in Medavakkam, Chennai. Led by Dr. Mangala Prema (PT), our clinic is dedicated to helping patients overcome pain, improve mobility, and regain their active lifestyle through personalized, evidence-based physiotherapy care.

With years of experience in treating orthopedic, neurological, and sports injuries, our goal is to provide holistic and result-oriented rehabilitation in a friendly and comfortable environment. At PhysioGlides, every treatment plan is designed after a detailed assessment of your condition, ensuring long-term recovery rather than just temporary relief.

We believe in combining clinical expertise with compassionate care, helping you move better, feel better, and live pain-free.

  • Patient-focused recovery plans
  • Evidence-based treatments
  • One-on-one dedicated sessions
  • Modern therapeutic equipment
